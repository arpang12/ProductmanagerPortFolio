Project Totoro

A Community Platform for Forest Spirits

This case study explores the creation of a decentralized communication network for woodland creatures, inspired by the magic of Studio Ghibli.

A Magical Overview

The goal was to enhance inter-species communication and resource sharing within the Whispering Woods. We aimed to create a delightful, intuitive experience for all inhabitants.

The Challenge

This is the problem statement we set out to solve. The forest spirits needed a way to communicate across vast distances without leaving their habitats vulnerable.

Our Creative Process

Step 1: Research

We observed the forest creatures in their natural habitats to understand their communication patterns.

Step 2: Design

Created intuitive interfaces that work for creatures of all sizes, from tiny soot sprites to giant Totoros.

Step 3: Develop

Built the system using natural materials and magical technology that blends seamlessly with the environment.
