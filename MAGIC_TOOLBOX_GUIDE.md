### Custom Image Upload
**NEW FEATURE!** You can now upload custom logos and images:

1. **Click the camera icon (ğŸ“·)** on any category or tool
2. **Select an image** from your computer (JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG)
3. **Automatic processing**: 
   - Images are automatically resized to optimal dimensions (128x128px)
   - Cropped to square format with centered composition
   - Optimized for web display
   - High-quality rendering maintained
4. **Remove images**: Hover over the image and click the Ã— button

**Image Requirements:**
- Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG
- Maximum file size: 5MB
- Best results: Square images or logos with transparent backgrounds
- Recommended: High-resolution images (they'll be automatically optimized)

### Image Upload Best Practices
1. **Company Logos**: Upload your employer/client logos for categories
2. **Tool Logos**: Use official tool/software logos for authenticity
3. **Transparent PNGs**: Work best for clean, professional appearance
4. **High Resolution**: Upload high-res images (they'll be optimized automatically)
5. **Square Format**: Square images work best, but any aspect ratio is supported
6. **File Size**: Keep under 5MB for quick uploads

### Best Practices
- **For Consultants**: Focus on strategic, analytical, and communication skills
- **For Tech Roles**: Highlight programming languages, frameworks, and cloud platforms
- **For Presentations**: Emphasize design tools, communication, and storytelling
